## About Me

Final-year student building across cloud, platform, backend, and product engineering. I spend most of my time shipping AI-assisted tools, healthtech experiments, automation-heavy workflows, and web products that are practical enough to be used, not just demoed.

My public work spans TypeScript, Python, JavaScript, frontend systems, backend integrations, and on-chain product ideas. I am particularly interested in platform reliability, developer tooling, applied AI, and products that sit at the intersection of software and real operational needs.
